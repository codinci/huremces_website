About Us

HUREM CONSULTANCY AND ENTERPRISE SERVICES LTD is a well-established outfit within Kenya and its presence is maintained through head office in Nairobi. HUREM CONSULTANCY AND ENTERPRISE SERVICES LTD is a company specialized in the provision of a wide range of HR Consultancy, Management Trainings, Recruitment & Selection, Research and Development services to Governments, Corporate Organizations, Private Sector, and Non-Profit making organizations.

Vision

Offer possible solutions to HR challenges experienced by people and organizations, fostering a better Africa and a better world of work.

Mission

To provide effective and efficient services that benefit users, exceed expectations, and present our company as honest and trustworthy at all times.

Code of Ethics

We assure all our Clients and stakeholders that we at HUREMCES we are publicly committed to providing the highest quality work at any other time we shall have an engagement with any of our clients.
